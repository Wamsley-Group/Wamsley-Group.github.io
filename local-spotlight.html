Featured Dayton Neighborhoods

The Oregon District

A vibrant historic district known for its charming brick streets, unique shops, and thriving arts scene. Perfect for those who love walkable urban living with character.

Oakwood

An upscale suburb with tree-lined streets, excellent schools, and beautiful historic homes. Ideal for families seeking a safe, established community.

Downtown Dayton

Urban living at its finest with loft apartments, entertainment venues, and dining options. Great for young professionals and those who enjoy city life.

Centerville

A family-friendly suburb with top-rated schools, parks, and shopping centers. Offers a perfect blend of suburban comfort and convenience.

Kettering

A well-established community with diverse housing options, excellent amenities, and strong community engagement. Perfect for families and retirees alike.

Beavercreek

A growing suburb known for its excellent schools, modern amenities, and proximity to Wright-Patterson Air Force Base. Ideal for military families and professionals.

Local Attractions & Amenities

National Museum of the U.S. Air Force

The world's largest military aviation museum, free to the public, showcasing the history of flight and military aviation.

The Greene Town Center

An outdoor shopping, dining, and entertainment destination featuring over 60 retailers and restaurants.

RiverScape MetroPark

A 32-acre park along the Great Miami River offering year-round activities, events, and beautiful outdoor spaces.

Five Rivers MetroParks

Over 16,000 acres of parks and natural areas with miles of biking and hiking trails throughout the Dayton region.
